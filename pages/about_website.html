About Our Website

Goal Of Our Website

Nowadays the demand for houses and flats is significantly increasing, and we wanted to make the
process of buying and selling Houses/Flats much more easier with help of this this Website. With
this Website, You can post the details of your house, for others to view and show interest in your
house and you can also view details of others houses and get interested in their houses.

How This Website Works

The Person who want to provide us the details of their house will be going to "Providing A House"
Page and fill up the google forms which will ask the details of their house, and these details will
be displayed in "Buy / Rent a House" Page from where others can view the house details which are
shown briefly in form of cards and when the person is interested in a specific house, they can click
on the card to view the full details of the house and contact the house owner.
